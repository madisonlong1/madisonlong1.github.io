Personal Interests - Film FAQ

Here are some of my thoughts on the industry. I find the blend of technology and storytelling has had a profound impact on how I view the world. While I am far from a filmmaker/major I enjoy this subject and have some thoughts to share!

Question Answer
What got you interested in film? I took a basic introduction to cinema course and it completly opened my eyes to how films blend all high forms of art
What's your favorite film genre? I'm particularly drawn to psychological horror and thriller films
Who are your favorite directors? Stanley Kubrick, John Carpenter, Ari Aster, and Jordan Peele - directors who masterfully blend technical precision with compelling narratives.
What's your all-time favorite movie? "The Shining" (1980) - Kubrick's psychological horror masterpiece showcases incredible cinematography, sound design, and Jack Nicholson's iconic performance.
Which other genres do you enjoy? I just love good storytelling. For example, have never been a fan of romance films but if its well made with a good story I will watch it!
What makes a good horror film? If the film makes you think about it three days later, thats a good sign
How do you choose what films to watch? I geek out with friends and take their recommendations
Do you have any film-related hobbies? I enjoy analyzing cinematography techniques
What's your favorite film from recent years? "Everything Everywhere All At Once" (2022) stuck with me for weeks. It has the strongest anti-suicide message I have seen so far.
How does film relate to your CS studies? It doesn't, I just really appreciate film as a hobby. CS is a field I enjoy for completly different reasons
Any plans to work in film technology? At my job, I work on processing and display, which is not necessarily film, but more data science
